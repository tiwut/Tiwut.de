Privacy Notice

This is a peer-to-peer (P2P) file transfer service. When you use this service, you establish a direct, encrypted connection to the other user's browser.

Your files are **not** uploaded to a central server. They are sent directly from your device to the other device. For the connection to be established, temporary and anonymous connection data is exchanged via a signaling server (Firebase). This data is deleted once the connection is closed.

By clicking "Accept", you agree to this process.
